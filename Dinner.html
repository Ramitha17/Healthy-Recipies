Dinner Recipes

Dinner is often the biggest and most formal meal of the day in many cultures. Historically, the largest meal was eaten around midday and called dinner. Over time, especially among the elite, it shifted to the evening. However, in some cultures, the word dinner simply means any meal of the day.
